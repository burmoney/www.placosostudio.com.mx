Our Creative Vision

Based in the heart of Zapopan, Jalisco, Placoso Studio is more than a clothing brand—it is a creative workshop where streetwear culture meets professional craftsmanship. We bridge the gap between high-end urban aesthetics and technical customization services.

What We Do

Our studio operates on two core pillars: Limited Edition Releases and Premium Customization. Every drop we produce is a result of meticulous design, ensuring that our community receives exclusive pieces that stand the test of time.

  • Custom Apparel: Specialized high-quality printing and embroidery for individuals and emerging brands.
  • Streetwear Curation: Limited hoodies, tees, and headwear designed with a premium urban edge.
  • Digital Arts: Integrating music and visual arts to influence the broader creative landscape.

Our Roots

Placoso Studio was founded as a hub for artists and creators. From our physical workshop at Mesa Colorada Poniente, we manage every aspect of the creative process, from the first sketch to the final stitch. Our 24/7 commitment to our craft ensures that we are always pushing the boundaries of what local streetwear can achieve.
