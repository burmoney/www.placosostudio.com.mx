Welcome to Placoso Studio — Mexico’s Rebel Streetwear Scene

Published on Feb 23, 2026

Hey everyone! We’re excited to introduce Placoso Studio, your new favorite streetwear brand straight out of Mexico. At Placoso, we celebrate the bold, rebellious spirit of urban life with unique designs that capture the energy and attitude of the streets.

Follow us to stay updated on our latest drops, behind-the-scenes looks, and street style inspiration. Join the Placoso movement and express your rebellious side with us!
